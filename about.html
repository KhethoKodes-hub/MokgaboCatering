Our Story

Established in 2009, Evacious Trading Enterprise & Catering has been a cornerstone of community empowerment in the Joe Morolong Local Municipality. Founded by Ms. Onkokame Evacious Damoense , our company has grown from a small catering service to a comprehensive enterprise offering diverse services across multiple sectors.

We are committed to delivering opportunities to the unemployed, unskilled, and historically disadvantaged while maintaining the highest standards of professional service. Our journey has been marked by consistent growth, community impact, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Mission

To deliver constant opportunities to the unemployed, unskilled, and historically disadvantaged through fair and progressive employment practices. We provide fair and progressive employment practices in accordance with the company's requirements for skills and the potential of its employees, reducing unemployment while generating sustainable returns on investments. These returns reward our members and secure funding for our continued growth and community impact.

Our Vision

To supply businesses in our area with comprehensive event & promotions catering, laundry & cleaning services, school feeding programs, and construction services with a skilled workforce supported by technology. We aim to be the preferred service provider in our region, known for reliability, quality, and our commitment to social upliftment and community development.
