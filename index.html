About Mokgabo

Established in 2009, Evacious Trading Enterprise & Catering has been a cornerstone of community empowerment in the Joe Morolong Local Municipality. We are committed to delivering opportunities to the unemployed, unskilled, and historically disadvantaged while maintaining the highest standards of professional service.

🏆 BBBEE Level 1 - 100% Black Female Ownership

Our Mission

To deliver constant opportunities to the unemployed, unskilled, and historically disadvantaged through fair and progressive employment practices, while generating sustainable returns and securing funding for continued growth.

Our Vision

To supply businesses in our area with comprehensive event & promotions catering, laundry & cleaning services, school feeding programs, and construction services with skilled workforce supported by technology.

Our Value System

Respect Client Rights

We respect the rights and confidentiality of all our clients.

Professional Service

We meet our clients' expectations of professional service excellence.

Integrity & Honesty

We operate with integrity, honesty and avoid conflicts of interest.

Non-Discriminatory

We operate in a non-discriminatory and non-partisan manner.

Empowerment
